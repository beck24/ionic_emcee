Step 1: Connect to WIFI

Connect this device to a WIFI network. The network does not need internet access. It can be a simple wireless router plugged into a wall, or a mobile hotspot provided by this or another device. All that matters is that this device and the display device are connected to the same network.

Swipe left for next instruction.

Step 2: Connect to Display

Tap on the "Scan Code" button. This will open the camera, use it to scan the QR code on the display device. On successful scan the connection between the devices will be established.


Example QR Code

Swipe left for
next instruction.

Step 3: Use Remote

On successful connection to the display device the remote functions will be displayed. This device will be able to control the timer in terms of timer duration, font size, timer start/stop/reset, and temporary popup messages.

A preview of the timer will show the approximate state of the display.

Swipe left for next instruction.

Step 4: Shut Down

When the device has finished being used you can disconnect it from the display device and restore the app to its initial state.

Tap on the display 5 times in rapid succession. A screen will pop up with a button to reset the application. Tapping this button will disconnect the device from the display and reset the app.
